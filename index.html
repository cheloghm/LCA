About Us

William Marrion Branham

William Marrion Branham (1909–1965) was a servant of God, called as a prophet to bring the End Time Message. His ministry was marked by divine healings, miracles, and a profound teaching ministry that unveiled the mysteries of the Bible.

Branham’s mission was to restore the hearts of believers to the apostolic faith and prepare the Bride of Christ for His imminent return. His teachings emphasize the simplicity of Christ and the return to the Word of God.

Local Christian Assembly (LCA) London

Local Christian Assembly (LCA) London is a fellowship dedicated to proclaiming the End Time Message brought by God through William Marrion Branham. Established in [YEAR], LCA serves as a spiritual home for believers seeking a deeper understanding of the Word of God.

The assembly is committed to nurturing believers, teaching the unadulterated Gospel, and preparing hearts for the Second Coming of Jesus Christ. We aim to provide a place of worship, spiritual growth, and fellowship for all.

What Happened in the Garden of Eden?
William Marrion Branham taught that the events in the Garden of Eden reveal the origin of sin, the fall of man, and the promise of redemption. He emphasized the serpent seed doctrine, highlighting the deception of Eve and its consequences on humanity.
The First Exodus
The First Exodus refers to God's deliverance of Israel from Egyptian bondage through Moses. This foreshadowed Christ's ultimate deliverance of His people from sin.
The Second Exodus
The Second Exodus refers to Christ’s deliverance of His Bride from the bondage of denominational systems and legalism, calling her back to the pure Word of God. Just as Moses led the Israelites out of Egypt, Jesus Christ leads His Bride into spiritual freedom.
The Third Exodus
The Third Exodus is the calling of the Bride of Christ out of worldly systems and into the fullness of the revealed Word. It signifies the spiritual journey of believers preparing for the Rapture through the End Time Message.
The Rapture
The Rapture is the catching away of the Bride of Christ to meet the Lord in the air. This event fulfills the promise of 1 Thessalonians 4:16-17 and signifies the culmination of the Bride’s journey in the End Time Message.
